# OPC Offer Sprint

Run a short sprint to create or sharpen a sellable offer.

## Workflow

1. **Collect proof**
   Ask for:
   - Target customer
   - Pain and trigger
   - Current workaround
   - Evidence from calls, reviews, support, or sales
   - Existing product or service
   - Delivery capacity

2. **Draft the offer**
   Use this shape:
   - Customer
   - Pain
   - Outcome
   - Mechanism
   - Timeframe
   - Proof
   - Price or pricing test
   - Risk reversal

3. **Route supporting skills**
   - Use `offer-creation` for value equation and packaging.
   - Use `pricing-strategy` for price architecture.
   - Use `copywriting` for landing page and sales copy.
   - Use `landing-page` or `landing-pages` for page structure.
   - Use `cold-outreach` for demand testing.
   - Use `unit-economics` when paid acquisition or delivery cost matters.

4. **Define the test**
   Pick one:
   - Five customer calls
   - Twenty outbound messages
   - Landing page smoke test
   - Paid ad creative test
   - Concierge delivery pilot

5. **Output**
   Produce:
   - Offer one-liner
   - Landing page sections
   - Outreach angle
   - Pricing hypothesis
   - Test plan
   - Kill or iterate criteria

## Guardrails

- Do not polish the offer before testing demand.
- Prefer a narrow painful segment over broad positioning.
- Keep the first test cheap and reversible.
