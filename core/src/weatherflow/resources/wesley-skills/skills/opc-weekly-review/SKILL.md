# OPC Weekly Review

Run a founder weekly review that ends with fewer priorities and clearer action.

## Inputs

Ask for or infer:

- This week's shipped work
- Customer conversations and objections
- Leads, trials, sales calls, conversions, churn, and revenue
- Content published and distribution results
- Product bugs, usage signals, and support themes
- Energy level and time available next week

## Workflow

1. **Score the week**
   Rate each area from 0 to 3:
   - Customer learning
   - Revenue progress
   - Product progress
   - Distribution progress
   - Operational leverage

2. **Extract evidence**
   Create three lists:
   - Facts observed
   - Assumptions changed
   - Open questions

3. **Find the constraint**
   Pick one constraint only:
   - Not enough qualified conversations
   - Weak offer
   - Weak onboarding or activation
   - Weak retention
   - Too much manual work
   - Cash/runway pressure

4. **Choose next week's bets**
   Select:
   - One growth bet
   - One product bet
   - One operations bet

5. **Write the plan**
   Output:
   - Week summary
   - Constraint
   - Three bets
   - Daily schedule
   - Kill criteria
   - Follow-up questions

## Guardrails

- Do not let the founder pick more than three meaningful bets.
- Tie each bet to a measurable signal.
- Prefer customer-facing work over internal polishing when evidence is weak.
